NAWIC

Starbuck has partnered with the National Association of Women in Construction (NAWIC) to support and advance women in the construction industry. Together, we’re creating more opportunities for women to enter, grow, and succeed in the trades.

Keep an eye out for a touch of pink at Starbuck — our pink excavators are driving conversations that champion women in construction.

Kinaway

We’re proud to partner with Kinaway, supporting the growth of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander businesses across Victoria. This partnership strengthens our commitment to meaningful engagement with Indigenous communities.

Our initiatives include:

  • RAP: Building cultural understanding and reconciliation.
  • Indigenous Procurement: Partnering with Indigenous-owned businesses.
  • Employment & Training: Creating opportunities for Indigenous individuals.
